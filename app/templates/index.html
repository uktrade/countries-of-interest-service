API

/api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest

Returns a list of company - country and sector of interest relationships for companies. These companies have expressed interest in the country, sector or country-sector combination. You can use the country, sector, service-company-id, , company-match-id, type and source arguments to filter the results.

e.g.

  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest
  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest?country=China
  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest?sector=Aerospace
  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest?type=interested
  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest?source=omis
  • /api/v1/get-company-countries-and-sectors-of-interest?country=China&sector=Aerospace
  • /api/v1/get-company-and-countries-sectors-of-interest?service-company-id=d0af8e52-ff34-4088-98e3-d2d22cd250ae
  • /api/v1/get-company-and-countries-sectors-of-interest?company-match-id=1
